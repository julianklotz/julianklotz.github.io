A Typographic Guide to Outdooractive

Vertical and Horizontal Spacing

Vertical spacing. Vertical spacing is based on the line height of 1.375rem (or 22px, considering a font size of 16px). The smallest possible unit is half a line height, i.e. 0.6875rem (11px). All vertical margins, paddings and line heights should be a multiple of half a line height.
Text areas and input fields are layed out in a way that a single-line input field is 36px high total, plus 8px margin-top to keep the baseline.

Horizontal spacing. Horizontal spacing is based on one “rem”, in our case 1rem equals 16px. Gutters, horizontal margins and paddings are based on this unit. Quarter steps may be used, e.g. 1.25rem (20px) is an acceptable horizontal spacing.

The impressive Rocks of Quiraing

Hiking Trail · Scottish Highlands

92,4 km 16:49  h 13459 m 12945 m 5 Bewertungen  mittel

This is an essential walk for any photographer as it passes though some of the most spectacular landscapes in Scotland. As part of the Trotternish ridge it has been formed by a massive landslip which has created high cliffs, hidden plateaus and pinnacles of rock. Choose a clear day and bring your camera.

The Quiraing walk is a loop, returning you to the same point (the carpark). It covers a distance of 6.8km, with the average time to complete the walk being 2 hours (with no stops). We have classed this walk as ‘Medium’ in length and ‘Hard’ for difficulty, check the rating guide for details.
