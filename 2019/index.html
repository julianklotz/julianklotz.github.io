Music, Concerts & Events

Music-wise, 2019 has been quite an exciting year.

The Tallest Man on Earth, singer and songwriter from Stockholm, Sweden, released his new album I Love You. It’s a Fever Dream. While still keeping his identity as a singer and songwriter, the album adds something new to his distinct voice and music. Some remarkable tracks are:

  • My Dear
  • What I’ve been kickin’ around
  • I’m a stranger now

The National put out a new record in May 2019, called “I am Easy to Find”. It’s not as ground-breaking as the 2017 album Sleep Well, Beast, but it still has some instant classics. I’ve been listening to Light Years quite a few times, both the studio and this live version.
