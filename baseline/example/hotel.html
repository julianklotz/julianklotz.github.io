Glencoe House

Hotel in the Scottish Highlands

Built in 1896 by Lord Strathcona in Glencoe , Glencoe House has now been transformed into an elegant 5-star suite hotel with 7 spacious suites (between 80 and 148 m²). Enjoy a stay at the Romantik Hotel Glencoe House in your very own private wing of a traditional British mansion, while providing all the technical amenities and 21st century comfort.

Getting There

By Car From The South

Glasgow – 2 hours

Edinburgh – 3 hours

Glencoe House is located in the heart of Glencoe. If you are travelling north on the A82 follow the signs for Glencoe Village. On entering Glencoe village, continue on the main street, you will pass the village shop on your left hand side, and then you will reach a small, hump back stone bridge which goes over the River Coe. Then turn second left, sign posted Glencoe Lochan, and stay left where the road divides. You will see Glencoe House ahead of you at the end of the drive.

Trail

The circular path is about 650 m long. Along the way there are various stations where you can learn about the moor and nature. Et al There are bird watching stations and a playground.

The trail is stepless.

It is a boardwalk with a width of 103 cm. The distance between the elements is 2 cm.

There is seating on the trail at regular intervals.

There are no slopes and sloping areas.
